# iTredVD Console Source of Truth

iTredVD is the SCI Commerce Console.

Its purpose is to create visibility for vendors in Africa and to act as the backend console for managing and monitoring all SCI applications in the commerce ecosystem.

iTredVD must support:

- Vendor onboarding
- Product intake
- Product-to-vendor linking
- Catalogue generation
- Storefront publication
- WhatsApp Access Hub tracking
- RPN and field operations
- Pricing plans
- Subscriptions
- License activation
- Console staff management
- Audit logs
- Read diagnostics
- Analytics and reports
- Monitoring of SCI POS, SCI Delivery, SCI Marketplace, and future SCI applications

iTredVD is the control tower of the SCI Commerce Operating System.
