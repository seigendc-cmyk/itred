# Vendor Visibility Source of Truth

The public-facing mission of iTredVD is to create commercial visibility for African vendors.

The platform must help vendors become:

- Discoverable
- Searchable
- Contactable
- Catalogue-ready
- Storefront-ready
- WhatsApp-commerce-ready
- Sector-indexed
- Product-indexed
- Regionally visible

Vendor visibility must support African sectors including motor spares, grocery, agriculture, hardware, property, vehicles, education, hotels, leisure, computers and phones, plumbing, professionals, jobbing services, pharmacy, transport, logistics, warehousing, clothing, spices, jewelry, perfumes, and general dealers.

Visibility is not only listing. It includes leads, enquiries, product views, catalogue views, WhatsApp interactions, phone call events, and analytics.
