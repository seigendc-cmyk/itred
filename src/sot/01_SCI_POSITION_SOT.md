# SCI Position Source of Truth

SCI means seiGEN Commerce Infrastructure / SCI Commerce Operating System.

SCI is an Africa-first commerce operating system designed to support business visibility, transaction control, licensing, monitoring, analytics, vendor discovery, POS operations, delivery fulfilment, access hub activity, and business intelligence.

SCI is not a single app. It is an ecosystem.

Core SCI applications may include:

- iTredVD / SCI Commerce Console
- SCI POS
- SCI Delivery
- SCI Marketplace
- SCI Access Hub
- SCI BI
- SCI Licensing and Activation
- Future SCI commerce applications

SCI must be built for African commercial realities, including informal-to-formal business growth, WhatsApp-led commerce, offline-first workflows, vendor discovery, RPN/field operations, licensing, subscriptions, and operational accountability.
